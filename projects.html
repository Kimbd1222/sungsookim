Projects

The Diagrammar of Quantum Magnusian

  • Developed a generalized diagrammatic framework for Quantum Magnusian structures.
  • Studied rooted and non-rooted graph structures and their combinatorial weights.
  • Implemented the public Mathematica package Quantum-Magnusian.

Root-Kerr Probe Dynamics

  • Investigated integrability properties of probe dynamics in root-Kerr systems.
  • Studied spin-order structures and obstructions to conserved quantities.

Magnusian Structures in Classical and Relativistic Scattering

  • Studied structures that previously appeared through the eikonal description.
  • Explored diagrammatic weights, non-rooted trees, and causal structures in relativistic scattering.
  • Worked toward a broader mathematical foundation of Magnusian diagrammatics.

CERN Summer Student Programme 2023

  • Worked on feasibility studies for detecting Ds+ → τ+ + ντ in pNe collisions at LHCb.
  • Selected as one of two representatives from Korea for the fully funded CERN Summer Student Programme.
