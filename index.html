About

My current interests include scattering amplitudes, particle physics, mathematical structures in field theory, and the diagrammatic foundations of classical and relativistic scattering.

A central direction of my recent work is to understand mathematical structures underlying scattering dynamics. In earlier studies of classical and relativistic scattering, these structures emerged naturally through the language of the eikonal. My current goal is to clarify their underlying mathematical foundations while keeping a broader perspective on scattering amplitudes, particle physics, and mathematical physics.
